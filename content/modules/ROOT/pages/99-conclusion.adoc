=== Customizing for Your Use Case

To adapt the platform for another IT process:

* Add a specialist agent for your process (e.g. PIA, RFP)
* Update the routing agent to route to your specialist
* Add MCP servers for your external systems
* Create a knowledge base with your policies
* Build evaluations for your business metrics
